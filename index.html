Do you have Questions?

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers!

Can I participate in multiple competitions?

Yes, you can register for multiple events as long as the schedules don’t overlap.

Make sure to check the schedule and manage your time wisely to compete in multiple events.

Will transport be provided?

Yes! We will arrange transport for participants to ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience.

You can relax and focus on the event while we take care of your travel needs.

Will participants receive certificates?

Yes, all participants will receive certificates, and winners will get special recognition and prizes.

Certificates will be issued upon successful participation and completion of the competition.

Can I register on the spot?

Yes, on-the-spot registrations are available, but slots are limited and subject to availability.

We recommend registering in advance to secure your spot and avoid last-minute rush.
